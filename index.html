Introduction

Hi, I am Gojo Satoru.

I'm a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High. People call me the strongest—and honestly, they’re not wrong. With the Six Eyes and my Limitless Cursed Technique, I can manipulate space itself, making me nearly untouchable.
